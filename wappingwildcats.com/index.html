About the Club

The Wapping Wildcats Volleyball Club was founded in 1994 by a small group of enthusiasts and is now well established in London's volleyball circle. The club has five London Volleyball League teams: two mens, two ladies and one mixed team in the Foundation League.

You can either come and enjoy one of our social sessions on Tuesdays or train with one of our teams and play more seriously as part of the London Volleyball League.

We are all about having fun, enjoying the game and - of course - socialising so we get involved in as many summer tournaments and pub crawls as we can.

The club is registered with the London Volleyball Association and Volleyball England .
